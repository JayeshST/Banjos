About Banjo's - The Food Chain.

Take two freshly baked buns with an appetizing stuffing of richly flavored quality and you have a Banjo!

Originated in 2017, from a passion to revolutionize in bun based cuisine, Banjos serves a diversified fast food menu. We are a Nashik based food chain which has grown tremendously only on the basis of our uncompromising quality. A constant admiration for our food from our customers has enabled us to expand our cuisine. Our first outlet on college road catered with a small range of delicious burgers, pizzas, sandwiches and a few beverages. Today we serve more than a 100 different dishes while retaining our original quality at 10 different locations. Every 6 months, we introduce innovation to our food to ensure that our menu gets better with time. You get to choose from a variety of soul-pleasing Banjos to savour. So what’s your Banjo?
